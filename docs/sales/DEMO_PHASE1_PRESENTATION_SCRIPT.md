# Phase 1 Demo Script (Simple Version)

Use this script to explain only Phase 1 features in simple language.

---

## 1) Start the Demo (first 30 seconds)

Say this:

"Today I will show only Phase 1 features.  
We are not showing future modules today.  
This demo is about what we are delivering now."

---

## 2) What is included in Phase 1

- Dashboard for each user
- Academic management
- Students
- Teachers
- Timetable
- Attendance
- Fees
- Payroll
- Audit log
- Settings and profile

If anyone asks for other modules, say:

"That is planned for next phase.  
Today we are showing current Phase 1 delivery."

---

## 3) Demo Flow (easy order)

1. Admin login and dashboard  
2. Academic setup (students and teachers)  
3. Timetable and attendance  
4. Fees  
5. Payroll  
6. Audit log  
7. Settings and profile  
8. Quick role switch (teacher/parent view if needed)  
9. Close and Q&A  

---

## 4) Full Talk Track (start to end)

## A. Opening (1 minute)

Say:

"Schools usually use many separate files and systems.  
Our Phase 1 brings daily school work into one platform."

Show:

- Login
- School access

Say:

"Now I will show real daily workflow from admin side."

## B. Admin Dashboard (2 minutes)

Show:

- Dashboard cards
- Recent Activity
- Upcoming Events

Say:

"Dashboard gives quick view of school operations.  
Admin can see important updates in one place."

## C. Academic Management (3 minutes)

Show:

- Class and section setup
- Student list
- Teacher list

Say:

"This is the base setup.  
Once this is correct, all other modules run smoothly."

## D. Timetable + Attendance (4 minutes)

Show:

- Timetable by class/section
- Mark attendance
- View attendance status

Say:

"This is daily classroom work.  
Teachers and admin can quickly manage timetable and attendance."

## E. Fees (4 minutes)

Show:

- Fee structure
- Payment entry
- Due and paid view

Say:

"Fees team can track collection clearly.  
It helps follow-up and reduces confusion."

## F. Payroll (3 minutes)

Show:

- Payroll list
- Salary process / payslip view

Say:

"Payroll is in the same system.  
So admin and accounts teams work faster."

## G. Audit Log (2 minutes)

Show:

- Audit records

Say:

"Every important action is tracked.  
Management can see who changed what and when."

## H. Settings and Profile (2 minutes)

Show:

- School/basic settings
- User profile update

Say:

"Settings and profile are simple and role-based."

## I. Role View (optional, 2-3 minutes)

Show:

- Teacher view (daily work)
- Parent view (limited visibility)

Say:

"Each role sees only what is needed.  
This keeps data safe and clear."

## J. Close (1 minute)

Say:

"This is our Phase 1 live scope.  
It covers core daily operations for school teams.  
Next phases can be added later step by step."

---

## 6) Simple FAQ (Admin / Principal / Owner)

## Rollout and training

## Q: "Our staff are not very technical. Can they use this?"
A: "Yes. Screens are simple. We also give role-wise training."

## Q: "How fast can we start?"
A: "After basic data setup, we can start Phase 1 in a planned rollout."

## Q: "Can we start with fewer modules?"
A: "Yes. We can go module by module inside Phase 1."

## Q: "Can you import our old Excel data?"
A: "Yes. We can import and verify data before go-live."

## Daily operations

## Q: "If internet is weak, what happens?"
A: "Daily workflow can continue as per rollout setup, and sync when stable."

## Q: "Can we control who sees fees and payroll?"
A: "Yes. Access is role and permission based."

## Q: "Can we check who changed records?"
A: "Yes. Audit log tracks important actions."

## Q: "Will this help principal and owner visibility?"
A: "Yes. Dashboard and reports give quick management view."

## Business value

## Q: "How does this help fee collection?"
A: "Clear due/paid view improves follow-up and collection."

## Q: "How does payroll help?"
A: "It reduces manual work and repeated data entry."

## Q: "What is the benefit for our school?"
A: "Faster daily work, better control, and less manual dependency."

## Q: "What after Phase 1?"
A: "Advanced modules come in next phases."

---

## 7) Questions You Should Ask the School (to move decision)

- "Which module should go live first in your school?"
- "Who will be your main coordinator for data validation?"
- "Do you want pilot first or full rollout directly?"
- "What reports do you want to see in first week?"
- "Shall we share a phase-wise go-live calendar?"

---

## 8) Final Closing Line

"If this Phase 1 scope matches your current priority, we can begin with this rollout and expand in next phases."
